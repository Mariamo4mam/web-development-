According to Coursera, the salary of a computer programmer in 2024 is: Entry-level: $62,572 per year Junior: $70,064 per year Mid-level: $78,125 per year Senior: $83,439 per year According to Course Report, coders who have completed a coding bootcamp earn an average of $69,079 per year. Programmers with a bachelor's degree earn around $93,000 a year, and senior computer programmers with a master's degree usually earn around $100,000 or more per year. In 2021, the median salary for computer programmers in the U.S. was $93,000 per year or $44.71 per hour. Individuals in entry-level positions typically earn far less than those with years of experience in the field.
